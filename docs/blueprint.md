# **App Name**: SupremeHealth

## Core Features:

- Role-Based Authentication: Secure authentication system supporting Patients, Doctors, Hospitals, and Ambulances, each with specific login flows and data access permissions, including phone/email OTP and role-specific verifications (NMR, NIN, Vehicle).
- Ambulance Dispatch System: Patients can request an ambulance, and the system will notify the nearest available ambulances via FCM; ambulance personnel can then accept the request and update the patient on the ETA.
- Automated Doctor-Patient Matching Tool: AI-powered suggestions for suitable doctors based on a patient's medical history and current needs, to suggest the best appointments.
- Telemedicine: Support direct telemedicine links for doctor-patient appointments and consultations
- Admin Verification Dashboard: Dashboard for administrators to verify NMR, NIN, and vehicle registrations, ensuring the legitimacy of healthcare providers and ambulance services. This may involve automated or manual methods.
- Medical Summarization Tool: Generative AI to automatically summarise a patient's medical history and records, creating a brief that can be reviewed by Doctors prior to a telemedicine appointment.
- Unified Dashboard Experience: A tailored dashboard experience for each role, providing quick access to relevant information, actions, and notifications, alongside a consistent UI.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: Deep indigo (#1E233B) to set a futuristic and premium tone.
- Primary color: Neon cyan (#00FFFF) to bring vibrant energy and futuristic accent.
- Accent color: Soft violet (#D09CFA) to highlight interactive elements and add depth.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for a geometric and contemporary style throughout the platform.
- Custom-designed icons with neon glows, following the futuristic glassmorphism theme.
- Glassmorphism effect applied to cards and panels with subtle 3D floating elements and soft blur backgrounds to give depth. Parallax scrolling effect on the landing pages to enhance the futuristic feel.
- Entrance animations with fade and translateY, hover effects with scale 1.02, ripple effects for buttons, success animations with checkmarks, and real-time updates for ambulance dispatch events.
